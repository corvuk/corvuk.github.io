"Solidarity forever" using an Arduino piezo buzzer.
Very rough, but for a quick foray, I'm happy how this turned out. Some of the melody is even recognisable!
This song is lifted from an established melody ("Battle hymn of the Republic") as is a lot of working class tunes of solidarity, but I specifically am referencing "Solidarity forever," written by Ralph Chaplin in 1915.
