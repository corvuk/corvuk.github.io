The Space Between (Bitsy)
As the first wave of the COVID-19 virus began to take hold globally, we could only watch as entire countries began to implement reactionary measures with ease. It was disturbing to see just how easily martial law could be enacted in the states it was enacted in. I wanted to explore deep-seated, cosmic horror - a terror that cannot be seen nor explained, as the era of household quarantine unfolded.

Interview with a psychopomp (Twine)
This unfinished game started with the idea of the mythological black dog. Known to be either malevolent or benevolent, I wanted to explore the narrative of a guardian spirit that acts as a psychopomp, or ferry of the dead.
The internet and the digital realm are just as real as the physical one we walk in, so I thought it would be fun to write about a psychopomp who has undertaken a job transferral to service digital space: protecting, guiding, subtly manipulating, and of course, ferrying souls onto the next world.
